ESP Sky Gauge

Mode

Radar live

Flight radar

Tip: right-click your home in Google Maps and click the coordinates to copy them. The location is shared with Weather mode. Aircraft positions come from adsb.lol, routes from adsbdb.com — the device fetches them itself, no PC needed.

Weather

Shows actual measurements from the nearest buienradar.nl weather station (KNMI network, NL/BE) at the location configured in the Flight Radar tab — saved once, used by both modes. Falls back to open-meteo.com outside station coverage. The device refreshes every 10 minutes.

Home Assistant

The device polls Home Assistant's REST API (/api/states/<entity>) with your token. Create a long-lived token in HA → your profile → Security. One entity per page — the round screen cycles through the pages you fill in. The value's unit comes from the entity itself. Use the device IP in the URL if homeassistant.local doesn't resolve. Token is stored write-only and never sent back to the browser.

Display

Captures exactly what's on the round display and opens it as a BMP in a new tab.

Network

Saving stores the credentials and reboots the device so it joins the new network. After it reboots, this ESP-Gauge hotspot disappears — reconnect your computer to your normal WiFi, then open http://esp-gauge.local (or the IP shown on the device).
